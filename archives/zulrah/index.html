Before proceeding, watch EVScape's Zulrah Guide for First Timers, then return here.

When I was first learning Zulrah, I struggled a lot with learning all of the rotations. EVScape's guide is great, but unfortunately there's a 3/4 chance that you will get the wrong rotation and have to teleport out. It is also difficult to get his guide to sync up perfectly with your kill, so he might be giving you instructions a couple seconds too early or too late. That is why I created this application. This web application allows you to listen to EVScape's instructional video up during your Zulrah kill very easily, regardless of which rotation it is, so that you can receive live instructions. Basically, you click "Start" and it begins a count-down. When the count-down gets to "GO", you begin your Zulrah kill. Once you know which rotation Zulrah is, you click "Rotation #X" and it will sync up EVScape's video with your current kill, at the exactly correct time. You will then be able to listen to EVScape instructing you where to run, what to pray, and which attack style to use.
